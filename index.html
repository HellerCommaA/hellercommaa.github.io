Discord Trade Text Generator

It's dead simple.

  • Hover over your item in game.
  • Press Ctrl+C.
  • Paste in `Paste Item Here` box.
  • Enter your want for this item.
  • Click `Add Item`.
  • Repeat till you have done all your items for trade.
  • Paste the generated text into the Discord trade channels!
  • A small link to this website is automatically included, please don't remove.
